✦ Awesomeness — Symmetry Brush
Choose a symmetry mode before drawing. Every shape you paint is automatically mirrored in real time:
• Horizontal — mirrors left↔right across the vertical center line.
• Vertical — mirrors top↔bottom across the horizontal center line.
• 4-Way — mirrors across both axes at once, producing perfect quad-symmetry. Great for mandalas and kaleidoscope patterns!
Works with all three shape types (squares, triangles, circles) and persists across clear/draw-picture actions.
